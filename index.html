DENT REPAIR

Vehicle dents can be just as expensive to repair as they are unsightly to look at. Traditional dent repairs are a precarious art, and good results aren’t always a guarantee. That’s why it’s a great idea to choose paintless dent repair in Austin, Texas, for your vehicle. The professionals at Lonestar Paintless Dent Removal have a business model of convenience, conservation, and affordability.

They will come to you during business hours, wherever and whenever fits best into your busy schedule. They use environmentally friendly techniques that don’t damage the paint or finish of your vehicle. More importantly, Lonestar Paintless Dent Removal is reasonably priced, and you’ll spend much less on paintless dent removal than you would pay for similar door ding repair at a traditional body shop.

HAIL REPAIR

Paintless Hail Repair is the process of gently massaging out hail damage from the underside of the dent. With specialized tools and lights (and years of training) a Technician can make a damaged vehicle look as if it were new again. In certain locations on the vehicle the damage will be pulled flat again from the outside using a special adhesive and solvents as to not damage the paint. The benefits of this process will be detailed below.

This paintless process is exactly how it sounds "paintless". The factory paint will be completely intact and unharmed unlike traditional body work which will replace the paint and cause the value of the vehicle to decrease. This process is also much faster than traditional body work and can be performed at almost any location.
